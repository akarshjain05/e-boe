# e-BoE (Electronic Bill of Exchange) System
## Project Overview & User Guide

Welcome to the **e-BoE System**! This platform was custom-built to modernize and simplify how your business handles billing, invoicing, and inventory management. 

Instead of relying on messy spreadsheets, manual calculations, or complicated accounting software, this system gives you a streamlined, all-in-one dashboard to manage your core business operations.

---

### What is e-BoE?
At its core, e-BoE is a professional invoicing and Bill of Exchange (BoE) management platform. It allows you to:
- Generate professional bills and invoices for your customers instantly.
- Keep track of the products and services you sell (including their GST, HSN/SAC codes, and stock levels).
- Manage your customer directory and view their purchase history.
- Track payments so you always know who owes you money and who has paid.

---

### Key Features Explained (Non-Technical)

#### 1. The Dashboard (Your Command Center)
When you log in, you are greeted with a beautiful dashboard that gives you a bird's-eye view of your business health. You can instantly see:
- Total revenue and pending payments.
- How many bills have been generated this month.
- Quick shortcuts to create a new bill or add a new product.

#### 2. Smart Billing & Invoicing
Creating a bill is designed to be as fast as possible. 
- **Auto-Fill Customers**: Type a few letters of a customer's name or GST number, and the system automatically pulls up their details. If they are registered on the official GST portal, the system can even fetch their real-time legal details automatically!
- **Auto-Fill Products**: When you add an item to the bill, the system automatically calculates the subtotal, applies the correct GST percentage, and updates the grand total.
- **Smart HSN/SAC Search**: The system is pre-loaded with over 21,000 official government HSN/SAC codes. If you don't know the description of a code, just type it in, and the system will auto-fill the official government description for you.
- **PDF Generation**: Once a bill is created, you can instantly download it as a professional PDF to print or email to your customer.

#### 3. Inventory & Product Management
This section is your digital warehouse. 
- You can add all the products (Goods) or services you sell.
- You can set their default price, unit of measurement (like kg, liters, or boxes), and default GST rate.
- **Edit on the fly**: If a price changes or you made a typo, you can quickly edit a product directly from the inventory list, and it will update immediately for all future bills.

#### 4. Customer Directory
Keep all your client relationships organized in one place.
- You can store their business names, GST numbers, phone numbers, and billing addresses.
- The system keeps a smart history, so the next time that customer returns, generating a bill for them takes only seconds.

#### 5. Payment Tracking
Never lose track of unpaid invoices. The system tracks the status of every bill:
- **Draft**: Bills you are still working on.
- **Unpaid/Pending**: Bills that have been sent but not yet paid by the customer.
- **Paid**: Cleared invoices.

---

### Why this system makes business easier:
- **Reduces Human Error**: Because taxes (GST) and totals are calculated automatically, there are no mathematical mistakes on invoices.
- **Saves Time**: Auto-filling customer GST details and product HSN descriptions turns a 10-minute invoicing task into a 30-second task.
- **Professional Appearance**: Customers receive clean, standardized PDF bills that make your business look highly professional and compliant.
- **Cloud-Based**: Because it runs on the web, you can access your business data from anywhere, whether you are at the office computer or on a laptop at home.

---

### How to get started?
1. **Add your Products**: Go to the Inventory section and add your most commonly sold items. Don't worry about memorizing HSN codes—the system's smart search will help you.
2. **Add your Customers**: As you get new clients, add them to the Customer directory. (Pro tip: Just enter their GST number and let the system fetch their details!)
3. **Create your first Bill**: Head to the Bills section, click "Create Bill", select your customer, add your products, and hit save!
